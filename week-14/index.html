Challenge

Create a system that would allow you to send multiple messages via a serial communication from ESP32 to your computer running a Processing sketch.

  • Q1: Will you make it for your own personal use or more general, usable by a larger group of people?
  • A1: Personal use
  • Q2: Is it mean for solo performance or multiple people?
  • A2: Solo
  • Q3: Will it be specific to a genre of music?
  • A3: Hip Hop
  • Q4: How do you select the parameters that you can manipulate, how do the ergonomics and the process of playing affect the layout and the functionality?
  • A4: Breadboard button = Kick, Mouse release on interface = Snare, Keys = Multi-Oscilator wave tones, Knob = Low Pass Filter, The inputs spread out through multiple controllers is not ideal for playing ergonomically.
